# Getting Started with TaskFriend

Welcome! Here’s how to make the most of your AI-powered assistant.

- Step 1: Log Your Tasks
    - Add everything — big projects, small todos, ideas.

- Step 2: Set Due Dates & Priorities
    - **Due**: Today, This Week, This Month
    - **Priority**: High, Medium, Low

- Step 3: Let TaskFriend Help You Decide
    - Ask TaskFriend for advice:
        - “What should I do next?”
        - “I’m stuck — help me”
        - “What’s due today?”
    - Discuss with TaskFriend on course of action:
        - "What is your suggestion?"
        - "Can I move X to Y, how would that impact today's schedule?"

TaskFriend will guide you based on your goals and workload.
