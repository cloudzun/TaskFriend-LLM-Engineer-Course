# TaskFriend Task Database

## What is TaskFriend?

TaskFriend is a helpful AI-assistant that helps with managing your daily tasks, and provides advice on time management and project scheduling.

## What are Tasks?

Tasks are the smallest denomination of work you can achieve in TaskFriend. You can set task type, due dates, status, and notes. This gives you full insight into your life and priorities, helping you better manage your time.
